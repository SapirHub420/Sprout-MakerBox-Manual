# SprouT MakerBox Component Catalog

This manual catalog provides a complete, easy-to-reference list of all the input and output components included with the SprouT MakerBox, along with a brief description of each one.

---

## 🔌 Input Components
Input components are physical controls and communication modules that detect user interaction or exchange data with external devices.

### Bluetooth Module
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Bluetooth Transceiver MLT-BT05.png" alt="Bluetooth Module" width="250">
</p>

A wireless communication module that allows the SprouT board to send and receive data via Bluetooth, enabling smartphone or wireless control.

---

### Button (Push Button)
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Push Button - Blue.png" alt="Push Button - Blue" width="250">
</p>

A simple tactile momentary switch that sends a digital trigger signal to the controller when pressed. *(Note: Available in Blue, Red, and Yellow variants).*

---

### Potentiometer
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Potentiometer.png" alt="Potentiometer" width="250">
</p>

A rotary dial knob that acts as a variable resistor, providing a precise analog input value to control variables like speed, volume, or angle.

---

## 📡 Input Components (Sensors)
These modules are designed to detect and measure physical or environmental conditions, translating them into electrical signals for the controller.

### Air Quality Sensor
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Gas Sensor MP-135 (Air Quality).png" alt="Air Quality Sensor" width="250">
</p>

Detects the presence and concentration of gas particles or pollutants in the surrounding air, outputting a variable analog voltage.

---

### DHT11 Sensor
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Humidity Sensor DHT11.png" alt="DHT11 Sensor" width="250">
</p>

A digital environmental climate sensor that measures both surrounding air temperature and relative humidity.

---

### IR (Infrared) Sensor
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Infrared Sensor.png" alt="IR Sensor" width="250">
</p>

An optical detection sensor that emits and detects reflected infrared light, commonly used for obstacle detection or line tracking.

---

### LDR (Light) Sensor
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Light Sensor.png" alt="LDR Sensor" width="250">
</p>

A photoresistor-based light sensor that measures ambient brightness and converts it into a variable analog value.

---

### Pressure Sensor (Capacitive Sensor)
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Capacitive Sensor.png" alt="Capacitive Pressure Sensor" width="250">
</p>

A touch-sensitive sensor that changes its output readings based on capacitive touch or applied physical pressure on its surface.

---

### Sound Sensor
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Sound Sensor (Noise).png" alt="Sound Sensor" width="250">
</p>

An acoustic module equipped with a microphone to detect surrounding environmental noise and sound triggers.

---

### Tilt Sensor
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Tilt Sensor.png" alt="Tilt Sensor" width="250">
</p>

A motion-activated orientation switch containing a small rolling metallic ball that completes a circuit when the module is tilted.

---

### Ultrasonic Sensor
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Ultrasonic Sensor.png" alt="Ultrasonic Sensor" width="250">
</p>

A non-contact distance measurement sensor that calculates range by emitting high-frequency sound waves and timing their echo reflection.

---

### Vibration Sensor
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Vibration Sensor.png" alt="Vibration Sensor" width="250">
</p>

A motion detector containing a small internal spring mechanism that registers physical impacts, jolts, and vibrations.

---

## 💡 Output Components
Output components receive commands from the main controller to interact with the external world using light, sound, mechanical movement, or electrical control.

### Buzzer (Tone Buzzer)
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Tone Buzzer.png" alt="Tone Buzzer" width="250">
</p>

An audio signaling component used to generate distinct beep sounds, alarms, or custom musical frequencies.

---

### DC Motor
*(Note: Refer to your kit accessories for the DC motor module hardware).*

A standard direct-current motor that translates electrical power into continuous high-speed rotational movement.

---

### LCD (1602 I2C Screen)
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - LCD I2C 1602 - Front.png" alt="LCD I2C 1602" width="250">
</p>

A liquid crystal character screen that displays text, numeric values, and customized real-time project updates directly from your code.

---

### LED (Light Emitter)
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Light Emitter - Red.png" alt="LED" width="250">
</p>

A highly efficient light-emitting diode used for status indicators, signal flashes, and visual lighting effects. *(Note: Available in Blue, Green, Red, White, and Yellow variants).*

---

### Motor Driver
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Motor Driver.png" alt="Motor Driver" width="250">
</p>

A power-handling circuit board that uses safe, low-power signals from the controller to drive and manage high-current DC motors.

---

### Relay (1-Channel Relay)
<p align="center">
  <img src="../images/PNG - TOP - SprouT - SENANG - Relay 1-Channel.png" alt="Relay 1-Channel" width="250">
</p>

An electromagnetic switch that allows low-voltage signals from the controller to safely control high-power and high-voltage electrical devices.
