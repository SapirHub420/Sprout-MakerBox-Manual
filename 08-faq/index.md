# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What age group is the SprouT Maker Box designed for?
The SprouT Maker Box is specifically designed for children aged 7 to 15 years old. The projects scale in difficulty, allowing kids to progress as they build confidence and skills.

---

### Does my child need prior coding experience?
No prior coding experience is necessary! The SprouT Maker Box starts with simple concepts and gradually introduces more complex ideas as your child progresses through the projects.

---

### What's included in the SprouT Maker Box?
Each box includes an Arduino-compatible controller, multiple sensor modules, LED modules, and connection cables.

---

### Is the SprouT Maker Box safe for children?
Yes! Safety is the top priority. The components are designed to be safe for children to handle, with no exposed electrical parts. All modules operate on low voltage that is safe for kids to work with under appropriate supervision.
